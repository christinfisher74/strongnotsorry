I've been a trainer for almost ten years. In that time I've worked with women of every lifestyle, every mindset, every season of life — and what I've learned is that everybody needs something different to be considered successful.

This isn't about what everybody else is doing. It's about what works for you. What makes you feel accomplished, complete, satisfied, and strong — based on where you are right now.

That's what longevity in fitness actually looks like. It's never quick fixes. It's a journey — something you do for the rest of your life. And you need to understand how to make it attainable for the rest of your life.

I'm here to meet you where you are. For every season.

  • 25–30g protein — protects your muscle tissue
  • 20–30g easy-digest carbs — fuels your glycogen
  • Keep fat low pre-workout — faster digestion

Recovery

You did that.

The work doesn't stop when the workout does. This is where your body actually rebuilds — give it what it needs.

Refuel within 60 minutes

30–40g rapidly absorbing protein — whey isolate or clean plant powder. 30–40g whole food carbs to replenish glycogen. Add electrolytes or sea salt to restore cellular hydration.

3 minutes of quiet

Sit in silence before jumping back into your day. Then 5 rounds of box breathing to shift your nervous system out of fight-or-flight and into repair mode.

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Repeat 5 times
